ingredients required for making grilled cheese sandwich

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 4 slices of cheese
    • cheddar, mozzarella, papper jack
  • 1 tbsp of butter

list with steps for creating grilled cheese

  1. Spread butter on bread and frying pan
  2. Place bread in frying pan and fry
  3. Add cheese on top of bread
  4. Cover with second slice of bread
  5. Turn over and fry for 2 minutes
